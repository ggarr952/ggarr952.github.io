About Lockeport

Lockeport is an island on the South Shore of Nova Scotia, Canada. The population of Lockeport is just over 500. 

Lockeport's economy is based on the tourism and fishing industries. Lobstering is the main income for the majority of Lockeport families. 

The quaint island has several shops and services as well as an elementary and high school. The sandy beaches and lighthouses of Lockeport are stunning.
